Iron-Deficiency Anemia Diet

Increase iron intake and pair it with vitamin C to boost absorption.

← All Health Conditions

✅ Eat More

  • Red meat (lean beef, pork liver) in moderate amounts
  • Malunggay leaves — rich in iron and vitamin C
  • Dilis and other small dried fish
  • Monggo, black beans, and other legumes
  • Fortified cereals and bread

⚠️ Limit / Avoid

  • Tea and coffee during or right after iron-rich meals (block absorption)
  • Calcium-heavy foods alongside iron-rich meals
  • Excessive dairy at iron meal times
  • Alcohol (interferes with iron metabolism)

💡 Practical Tips

  • Pair iron foods with vitamin C (calamansi, guava, pepper) to double absorption
  • Cook in a cast iron pan — small amounts of iron leach into food
  • Space tea and coffee 1 hour away from iron-rich meals
  • Get a blood test to confirm iron levels before supplementing
📊 Calorie needs are normal; focus is on iron-rich food pairing and absorption.
